How to Prepare for GHC 2022

After 2 years of being virtual, the Grace Hopper Celebration is planning to go back to the in-person format, here’s all the details you’ll need to plan.

GHC next year will be Sep 20- 23 in Orlando, Florida. As a reminder, the format could change based on public health and it may go virtual again, but so far its looking like Orlando

From the booking we know there will be approximately 25,000 tickets; I’m rounding down because some spots are reserved for organizers. This makes it the size of the 2019 Grace Hopper Celebration which was also held in Florida. This is down from the 30k in 2020 the virtual year, but we all know how that went.






My Experience in stay for GHCI

Let’s talk hotels, from 2017 to 2019 AnitaB.org upped their shuttle game. Originally there were just shuttles to locations near the conference center, but in 2019 they added ones that took you to the SeaWorld hotels and the not Disney World proper — but the affiliated Disney world hotels. They’re more of a shlep to get to, but the wifi will likely be better if you need it for work

In 2017 I stayed at one of the hotels attached to the conference center, and its wifi was so weak I could only work on homework at 2 am and otherwise I had to go to to the expo halls. I thought the wifi was stress tested because of all the techies, but reading the Yelp comments a dentist’s convention had the same problem. According to Yelp reviewers, a lot of the Orlando hotels struggle with bed bugs so do your research before booking.

You could wait until closer to the conference to get a slight discount as part of their block rates, but the discount wasn’t that big. I think I got $20 off a room that’s normally $250. If you book with their blocking system you are charged when you book, not when you check in to your hotel. They also have a term/ condition that says if you do not check in the day your reservation is supposed to start, you forfeit your reservation. In 2019 this became a problem for me because American Airlines canceled my flight day of. That’s a story for another day though. I’ve found if you work at a company that has hotel discount you are probably better booking with that.

I hope this helped you plan, and if you have any questions let me know in the comments below. Have a great day everyone and hope to see you at Grace Hopper next year!
